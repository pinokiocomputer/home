How it works

Four simple ways Pinokio helps you discover, launch, manage, and automate apps locally.

1

Discover open source apps

Find community-built AI apps, agents, and local services in one searchable directory before installing anything.

2

1-click launcher

Browse a growing library of one-click launchers for AI apps, agents, and tools. It's like running your own personal web services on your PC.

3

Install multiple versions

Install, switch between, and manage multiple copies or versions of the same app side by side.

4

Use agents with your apps

Any AI agent can control your apps, build workflows or apps, or answer questions about your app.
